### Audit of Li's proof

The arXiv v1 TeX source was inspected directly. Its Part I proof of the Erdős 593 classification appears correct, conditional on its cited classical inputs. No fatal gap or counterexample was found. Three minor formal revisions would improve it: state \(m\ge2\) in the Berge-cycle definition, restrict the triple-system consequences of its isolated-vertex lemma to triple systems, and dispatch the empty system explicitly in the finite bridge-decomposition proposition. None affects Li's main classification theorem. This audit is comparative only and is not used as a premise in the present proof.
